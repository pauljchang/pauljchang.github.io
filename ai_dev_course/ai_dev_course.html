About this course

This course teaches AI-assisted development as a real engineering workflow — not a shortcut. Each lesson is built around a working app that grows in complexity, and every prompt is provided so students can focus on reading and evaluating the output rather than guessing how to phrase requests.

The course assumes no prior web development experience. A browser and a free account on Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini are the only requirements.
