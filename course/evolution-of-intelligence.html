About This Course

Evolution of Intelligence is a self-paced, browser-native AI/ML curriculum. Every lesson is a single self-contained HTML file — no install, no login, no build step. Open a file and start learning.

The course covers the full arc of AI history: from the first perceptron in 1958 through expert systems, statistical learning, deep learning, large language models, and the open frontier of autonomous agents and efficient inference.

Each lesson includes a conceptual explainer, an interactive demo, graded lab exercises, a terminology glossary, and discussion questions.

How It Was Made

The curriculum design, lesson sequencing, pedagogical choices, interactive concepts, and overall vision for this course are the work of its human author.

The lesson pages themselves were generated with Claude Sonnet 4.6 by Anthropic — an AI assistant that wrote the HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SVG graphics, demo mechanics, and exercise content from detailed specifications and iterative feedback.

Think of it this way: the ideas, steering, and judgment are human. The implementation is a collaboration between human and AI.
