Micro-Transformer

How large language models actually work, taught by building a tiny one from scratch — in the browser, live, no installs, no PyTorch. Every lesson trains a real neural network (hand-rolled forward and backward passes, no libraries) on a made-up "caveperson" language small enough to read the entire training set in one glance.

The same math that runs inside billion-parameter models — embeddings, position, attention, gradient descent, autoregressive generation — shows up here at a scale you can actually hold in your head, watch train step by step, and poke at until it breaks.
